Quick Overview of commonly used 3D Printing Materials!

One of the largest advantages of 3D printing comes from being able to leverage different material properties. We'll be reviewing some of the common materials used by consumer-level printers. This will help you get an idea of what options you have as a designer for utilizing these materials as well as consider some of the challenges that come from trying to print them.

Material Strength Cost/Spool Printability Unique Characteristic Example Applications
PLA Weak $11 Easy Cheap Prototypes
PETG Strong $20 Intermediate Resist surface wear Mounths/Fixtures
